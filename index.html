Welcome to Detabi Travel, where unforgettable journeys and breathtaking moments await. Our name, inspired by the Japanese words "de" (departure) and "tabi" (travel), encapsulates the very spirit of our brand - the excitement of embarking on extraordinary adventures.

Logo of Detabi Travel

At Detabi Travel, our logo holds profound meaning, embodying the spirit of wanderlust and boundless exploration. Let us introduce our iconic symbol: the felix.

Our felix logo represents the epitome of curiosity, encapsulating the joy of traversing the globe. Just like the agile felix, we embrace the thrill of venturing into uncharted territories, discovering hidden gems and immersing ourselves in diverse cultures.

As your trusted travel companion, Detabi Travel invites you to embark on a journey of limitless possibilities. Our felix logo serves as a constant reminder of the exhilaration and freedom that travel brings, inspring you to embrace new experiences and create cherised memories.
